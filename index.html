About Me

I earned my Bachelor of Computer Science (Honours) from the University of Information Technology (UIT), Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City (VNU-HCM). Presently, I work as a Research Assistant while pursuing my master's degree at the Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD) being advised by Prof. Ngai-Man Cheung.

My research interest is to develop Responsible AI, specifically in developing robust AI systems resilient to prevalent threats like adversarial attacks, model inversion attacks, and out-of-distribution shifts.
